Evidence Nodes

Background

A claim is shown to hold by its argument and evidence. Evidence must clearly show that its parent claim holds according to its context and assumptions. They may be individual pieces of analysis, evidence, results of audit reports, references to design material, etc.

Application

To add an evidence node to the case argument model, right-click on a Claim or Argument node, choose Add Child... from the pop-up menu, and choose Solution. Select the new evidence node and then complete its properties in the property sheet form.
